    Sacred Practice

    How to work with your crystals

    Your crystal is more than jewellery — it's a tool. Follow this ritual to activate its full energy and keep it working for you.

    01
    🌊

    Cleanse

    Before wearing your crystal for the first time, cleanse it. Hold it under running water for 60 seconds, or leave it in sunlight for an hour, or place it overnight on a selenite plate. This removes any stale energy from transit.

    02
    🕯️

    Set Intention

    Hold the crystal in your palms, close your eyes and breathe deeply three times. Speak your intention aloud or in your mind — be specific. "I am open to love," "I attract abundance effortlessly." Feel it in your body.

    03
    ✨

    Wear & Receive

    Wear your bracelet on your left wrist to receive its energy, or right wrist to project and give its qualities outward. Re-cleanse monthly under the full moon. The more you connect with it consciously, the stronger it works.
